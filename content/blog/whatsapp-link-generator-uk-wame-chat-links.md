## 📐 Formatting UK +44 WhatsApp Links

To format a WhatsApp link for the UK manually, append your mobile number (excluding the leading zero) to the +44 country code:

$$\text{URL} = \text{"https://wa.me/447"} \mathbin{\|} \text{RemainingDigits}$$

Important formatting rules:
*   **Remove the Leading Zero**: UK mobile numbers start with `07`, but when formatting the link, you must discard the `0`. For example, `07700 900077` becomes `447700900077`.
*   **Remove All Formatting**: Do not include spaces, hyphens, or parenthetical area codes.
