## 📐 How to Format US WhatsApp Links

To create a direct click-to-chat link for US phone numbers, format the URL as follows:

$$\text{URL} = \text{"https://wa.me/1"} \mathbin{\|} \text{AreaCode} \mathbin{\|} \text{SubscriberNumber}$$

Important formatting rules:
*   **Omit Special Characters**: Do not include parentheses, dashes, or spaces (e.g., `+1 (555) 019-2834` must be written as `15550192834`).
*   **Verify WhatsApp Registration**: Ensure the number has been registered and activated on the WhatsApp or WhatsApp Business app before sharing the link.
