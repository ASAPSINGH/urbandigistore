Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 18, 2026. Learn how Urbandigistore maintains complete local data confidentiality.

1. Privacy-First Local Sandbox

Urbandigistore is built on a decentralized local architecture. Unlike traditional online utility tools, all file conversions, JSON formatting, URL calculations, and financial modeling are performed entirely within your web browser's local sandbox memory. No files, documents, text blocks, or parameters are uploaded to external servers.

2. Zero Server Logs & Data Retention

We do not maintain databases, server caches, or logs containing details of your processed files. Our hosting infrastructure only registers standard network request headers necessary to fetch application assets (HTML, CSS, JS) and log basic server uptime parameters. Your business payloads remain strictly confidential on your own machine.

3. Newsletter Subscriptions

If you voluntarily submit your email address via our newsletter form, we store this information securely to deliver operational checklist guides, technical updates, and platform tools. We do not sell, rent, or lease our subscriber list to third parties, and you can unsubscribe at any moment via the link in the footer of our newsletters.

4. Cookies and Analytical Tools

We utilize lightweight Google Analytics integrations to track high-level traffic indicators (e.g., page views, session duration, regional distributions) for SEO refinement. These services process anonymous client data and do not have access to any content parsed inside individual tools.

5. Third-Party Integrations

Our application templates include links to partner services (e.g., Canva). Once you click these affiliate referrals, your interactions are governed by their respective privacy standards. We suggest reviewing their documentation prior to purchasing or using their platforms.
